Hevo Starter plan vs Fivetran Standard plan




Hevo stands out from Fivetran due to its real-time processing approach in data replication. By leveraging Kafka for data processing, Hevo ensures that each individual row from the source system is replicated in near real-time to the destination. This means that changes in the source data become immediately visible in the destination, providing users with up-to-the-moment insights and enabling faster decision-making. In contrast, Fivetran utilizes batch processing, where data is replicated only after an entire batch is processed, potentially introducing delays and limiting the immediacy of data availability. Hevo's capability to replicate data on a row-wise basis in real-time gives it a distinct advantage for scenarios where timely and accurate data replication is crucial for business operations and analytics.
